Services

Career/Education Direction Coaching

If you're unsure what's next on your journey or feel pulled in multiple directions, we'll slow things down and create space to clearly define your path. Together, we'll explore your strengths, interests, and goals so you can move forward with confidence and intention.

Resume Building & LinkedIn Profile Optimization

Together, we'll craft a results-driven resume and LinkedIn profile that clearly communicate your strengths, showing your true potential. We will position your experience in a way that catches recruiter's attention and resonates with hiring managers.

Job Coaching & Interview Preparation

Interviews don't have to feel intimidating. We'll prepare together so you feel grounded, confident, and ready to communicate your strengths clearly. From mock interviews to job search strategy, I'll be there to support you every step of the way.

Cover Letter Writing

A strong cover letter adds depth to your application and can be the foot in the door that makes all the difference. We'll create thoughtful, personalized letters that connect your experience to the role, helping hiring managers recognize that you are the best fit for the position.
